Is This Right for You?

Who This Assessment Is Designed For

This is a general guide. Final eligibility for treatment requires a provider evaluation. A free consultation helps determine the right path for your specific situation.

This Is For You If...

  • Are 50 or older with chronic knee pain
  • Have been diagnosed with osteoarthritis, degenerative joint disease, or similar conditions
  • Have tried other treatments without lasting relief
  • Want to explore options before committing to knee surgery
  • Have Medicare or private insurance
  • Are open to a free consultation to understand your options

This Is Not For You If...

  • Have an active knee infection or acute injury requiring immediate emergency care
  • Have already had successful knee surgery with good results
  • Are not ready or willing to discuss treatment options at this time
  • Are specifically seeking a surgical consultation

Treatment eligibility is determined by a licensed provider during your evaluation. This criteria is for general guidance only and does not constitute a medical determination.
